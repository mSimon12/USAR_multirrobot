Keyboard commands:

Reading from the keyboard  and Publishing to Twist!
---------------------------
Moving around:
   u    i    o
   j    k    l
   m    ,    .
For Holonomic mode (strafing), hold down the shift key:
---------------------------
   U    I    O
   J    K    L
   M    <    >

t : up (+z)
b : down (-z)

q/a : increase/decrease linear speed
w/s : increase/decrease angular speed
e/d : end/error teleoperation

CTRL-C to quit
